Help & Guidelines

Getting Started

1. Select your preferred savings frequency (daily, weekly, or monthly).

2. Set your savings target amount for each period.

3. Choose a start date and duration for your savings plan.

4. Click "Generate Plan" to create your personalized savings schedule.

Tracking Your Savings

1. Mark each period as "Saved" when you've deposited the amount.

2. The tracker will automatically calculate your cumulative savings.

3. Add notes for each period to record any important details.

Setting Reminders

1. Use the reminder section to set up savings alerts.

2. Choose a time and frequency for your reminders.

3. Click "Set Reminder" to save your preferences.

Exporting Data

1. Click "Export to PDF" to create a printable report of your savings.

2. The PDF will include your savings details and summary, with your name in the filename.
